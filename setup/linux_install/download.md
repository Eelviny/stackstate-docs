StackState prefers Kubernetes!<br />In the future we will move away from Linux support. Read the [Kubernetes migration guide](link_to_guide.md) to find out more.
{% endhint %}

StackState and related software are distributed through our download website. The software distribution website is located at [download.stackstate.com](https://download.stackstate.com).

It is required to have a license key to download from the distribution website. A license key can be obtained by getting in touch with us, please visit our [contact](https://www.stackstate.com/company/contact/) page for more information.
